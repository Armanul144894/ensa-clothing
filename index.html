Who We Are

Built on Experience,
Driven by Excellence

ENSA Clothing Ltd. is a global apparel sourcing agent in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Established in 2015 by a team of well experienced garment professionals, the company is well entrenched in providing clients with the highest quality garments accompanied by on-time delivery across all product categories.

ENSA Clothing has strongly established its repute through a robust sourcing network in the production of a variety of garments — knit, woven and sweaters — with a specialised division dedicated to handicraft items. We guarantee delivery of a wide range of apparel products within an extremely reasonable timeframe while strictly maintaining quality requirements and specifications.

Our team of highly skilled merchandisers and quality controllers are committed to ensuring maximum client satisfaction. Our partner factories are fully compliant with Accord, Alliance, ISO 9001, WRAP, BSCI, OCS and Oeko-Tex certificates, serving renowned importers from Poland, France, Italy, Germany and beyond with a current annual turnover of approximately US$18M.

What We Do

Full-Spectrum Sourcing Services

ENSA is a best global fashion sourcing specialist, creating pieces of apparel and embellishments bar none. We work legitimately and directly with buyers and designers alike — from concept to mass production. Our full range of services and expertise covers fabric and trims sourcing, merchandising, garment development, global trade, and delivery. We always source the best fashion at minimum price for maximum value.

From design concept to delivered shipment — our teams manage every stage of your supply chain with precision.

01
Bulk Manufacturing

Our team members exert great effort into designing and manufacturing the best products — bound to win the hearts of our buyers. We perform daily random checks from bulk production, tested through our in-house laboratory. Our merchandising team supervises goods in factory at every stage to ensure full compliance with customer requirements.

02
Fabric Quality & Durability

At ENSA we are on a mission to offer our clients the best fabrics to make products consumers can trust. We combine on-the-ground experts for quality inspections, supplier audits, certification and in-house lab testing with a digital platform for complete accuracy, visibility and compliance intelligence — as per the best standards our customers require.

03
On-Time Delivery

Our main focus is delivering all goods on time as per the committed date — and we have a 94% success rate. Our product team works with a lean line layout for maximum output with minimum effort. We have a strong planning team working from the bottom line, always strictly controlling delivery dates.

04
Product Development

Our strong design department ensures the profitability and quality of mass-produced fashion items. Product development at ENSA is a team effort — overseeing decisions where design meets marketing, developing items to customer specifications, and following current seasonal colour charts with maximum effort for our customers.

05
Social Responsibility

We believe every business must act in a manner that benefits society, not just the bottom line. At ENSA we contribute to education, medical support, and the welfare of homeless people. Our company strictly follows Detox for Zero Hazard principles to protect human life and the environment — because it is our responsibility.

06
Quality Guaranteed

ENSA gives you our quality guarantee. We offer quality control and assurance services at every stage of your apparel supply chain — from source to shelf. With strong off-the-shelf customisable inspection checklists, our soft-line testing expertise ensures the safety and quality of your apparel. We guarantee quality as per customer AQL standards.
