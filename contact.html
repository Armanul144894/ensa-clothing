Frequently Asked Questions

Our MOQ varies by product category. For knit and woven garments it is typically 300–500 pcs per style per colour. For sweaters, 200 pcs. For handicraft items, we can accommodate as low as 50–100 pcs for boutique buyers.
Standard lead times from sample approval to delivery are: Knit/Woven 45–60 days, Sweaters 60–75 days, Activewear 45–60 days, Handicraft 45–60 days, Lingerie 55–70 days. We maintain a 94% on-time delivery rate.
Yes. We can provide fabric swatches, colour lab dips, and pre-production samples before bulk production begins. Sample costs vary and are typically deducted from bulk order costs upon confirmation.
Our partner factories are fully compliant with Accord, Alliance, ISO 9001, WRAP, BSCI, OCS, and Oeko-Tex certifications. We can provide relevant compliance documentation for any order upon request.
Absolutely. We specialize in private label and OEM production. We can apply your brand's woven labels, care labels, hangtags, and packaging as specified. Custom retail-ready packaging is also available.
Our quality team performs daily random inspections on the production floor, in-house lab testing on fabrics and finished goods, and final AQL inspections before packing. Third-party inspection agencies (SGS, Bureau Veritas) can also be accommodated.
Our main markets are Poland, France, Italy, and Germany, with a growing presence across Europe. We currently serve buyers in 7 countries and are open to partnerships with buyers in new markets.
