Leadership & Vision

Mr. Enamul Kabir

Managing Director, ENSA Clothing Ltd.  ·  Est. 2015

"Maximum output through minimum efforts." This mantra has guided every decision across my 30-year career in the RMG industry — from implementing out-of-the-box sourcing strategies to building ENSA into a globally trusted apparel partner. Our buyers' interests are always at the centre of everything we do.

An industry maven with a career spanning 30 years, Mr. Enamul Kabir is the Managing Director and driving force behind ENSA Clothing Ltd. Born in Bangladesh, he earned his reputation as a business leader in the ready-made garment (RMG) industry — a career that began in 1995 with a desire to make his mark. He is credited for implementing many out-of-the-box strategies and techniques to source the best fabrics and have them produced at the best possible production units at competitive prices.

With his vast knowledge in textiles and RMG, Mr. Kabir set up his own business in 2006. Through the patronage of many foreign buyers, his reputation gained further recognition. Constantly in pursuit of finding the most innovative ways to best serve his buyers' interests, he lives by his mantra: "maximum output through minimum efforts". In 2015 he founded ENSA Clothing Ltd., channelling three decades of expertise into building Bangladesh's most trusted sourcing agency.

What We Do

Our Social Programmes

ENSA's social initiatives span education, community welfare, cultural events, and workforce wellbeing — contributing to a better Bangladesh for everyone.

Education
Free School Dresses to Rural Areas

ENSA donates free school uniforms to children in rural and underserved communities across Bangladesh. By removing the financial barrier of school dress, we help ensure more children can attend school regularly and focus on their education.

Education Support Initiative
Team Welfare
Annual Outing to Sylhet

Every year, ENSA organises an annual team outing to Sylhet — Bangladesh's beautiful northeastern tea garden region. The trip strengthens team bonds, rewards dedication, and provides well-deserved rest and recreation for all ENSA staff and their families.

Staff Wellbeing & Team Building
Community Commerce
ENSA Clothing Outlet

The ENSA Clothing Outlet brings quality garments directly to local communities at affordable prices. Providing access to well-made, fairly priced clothing supports both consumers and the broader textile ecosystem that ENSA is proud to be part of.

Affordable Quality Clothing
Community Leadership
Lions Club — Dhaka Fareast

Our Managing Director proudly serves as President of Lions Club Dhaka Fareast, District 315 A3. Through this role, ENSA participates in humanitarian activities ranging from eye care camps and blood donation drives to disaster relief efforts across Dhaka.

Humanitarian Service
Healthcare
Medical Support for Workers

ENSA contributes to healthcare programmes for factory workers and their families. This includes support for medical treatment, preventive health checks, and partnerships with community health initiatives that ensure workers have access to the care they deserve.

Worker Healthcare Programme

Community & Cultural Events

Beyond structured programmes, ENSA regularly participates in and sponsors cultural celebrations, Eid gatherings, local festivals, and community welfare events — bringing people together and reinforcing ENSA's role as a positive force in Dhaka and beyond.
